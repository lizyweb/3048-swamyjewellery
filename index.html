About us

WE'RE A PREMIUM JEWELLERY STORE DEDICATED TO BRINGING YOU THE BEST OF THE BEST.

Swamy Jewellery began its journey as a gold and silver retail showroom in 1998 in Vadavalli, Coimbatore. Our vision is to provide an exclusive jewelry shopping experience to our valued customers, with an unwavering commitment to quality and timely delivery. We take pride in introducing trend-setting designs regularly, keeping pace with the ever-changing fashion and jewelry market. We have evolved into one of the most trusted and favored jewelry shops for the people of Coimbatore, which we believe reflects our integrity and dependability over the past 25 years of business.
